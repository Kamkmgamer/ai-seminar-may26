# PRODUCT.md

## Register

brand

## Product Purpose

AI in 2026 is a bilingual learning site for students who want a practical, current map of modern AI. It introduces agents, safe workflows, tool choices, and project-building as one coherent learning path.

The landing page should create momentum and credibility. It should feel like a designed field guide for a fast-moving technical world, not a generic AI SaaS page.

## Users

- Non-technical or early technical students.
- Students attending a 2-3 hour workshop.
- Arabic and English readers, with Arabic using a light Sudanese voice.
- Students who need confidence, orientation, and practical next steps.

## Brand Voice

- Clear, alert, and generous.
- Practical without being dry.
- Confident about AI, but careful about safety and hype.
- Student-first: explain the landscape without making the reader feel behind.

## Strategic Principles

- AI literacy first, tool setup second.
- Explain fast-moving details through current references instead of fixed promises.
- Make safety visible without making the experience scary.
- Treat Arabic as a first-class experience with RTL support.
- Use visual energy to make the site memorable, but avoid generic neon robot slop.

## Anti-References

- Generic dark AI SaaS hero with glowing orb and vague productivity claims.
- Nested dashboards and card grids.
- Blue-purple gradient text.
- Stock robot heads, brain icons, or random circuit-board decoration.
- Overly academic pages that look like a PDF converted to a website.
