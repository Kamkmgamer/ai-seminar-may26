# Welcome to learning AI in 2026

AI in 2026 is not one chatbot or one magic app. It is a changing ecosystem of models, agents, tools, workflows, risks, and opportunities. This workshop gives you a practical map.

## What this course is about

You will learn how to think about AI tools right now: what they can do, what they should not do, how to compare them, and how to use them to build real things without losing control.

## What you will learn

- What AI agents are and where they are useful.
- How to prepare your computer for modern AI workflows.
- How to use OpenCode as the main hands-on agent.
- How to publish your code without leaking secrets.
- How to deploy the finished website.

## Your first checkpoint

By the end of the core path, you should understand the 2026 AI landscape better and have a deployed portfolio as proof that you can turn AI assistance into real output.
